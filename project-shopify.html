Building a learning app for Shopify

Iterated and improved the UX of one of Shopify's learning apps

My role

  • Usability Tester
  • UX Team of one

1. Sticky notes

Through exploration I was able to get a hold of the app and its place in Shopify's ecosystem.

2. Sitemap and Flows

With the content mapped, I was able to structure the flows and see how the user would navigate through its function. Some problems were apparent at the early tests we conducted such as users having no clue of what to do when they landed in the course feed section.

3. Sketches

Paper prototyping was a great tool to test my designs. I quickly iterated with them and got interesting results. Our course feed was one of them.

Amazing Job!

- Angie Fahlman

5. Final design

The final result displayed a new type of course feed, much like Duolingo and Memrise. Through testing we were able to see that having a clear path with the option to look for courses in categories was a great way to improved user experience.
