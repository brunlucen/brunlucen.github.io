Hi! I'm Bruno Lucena, a Brazilian Designer living in Ottawa. Good design is good business

Over the years, I've learned quite dramatically that "the obstacle is the path", and this led me to approach work knowing that great products are not just a matter of individual expertise (that's just the starting point), but also a matter of how your team works together to overcome the many challenges that will surely present themselves on the way.
